# Clusta

Clusta is a desktop application for organizing and managing your images for your projects or your personal collection.

It provides a user-friendly interface to import your images, categorize them, and easily search for them when needed.

Additionally, Clusta includes an Excalidraw window, allowing you to drag your images in and create scenes for your projects and notes. This is especially useful for artists, designers, and anyone who needs to visualize their ideas.

## Features

- **Image Organization**: Import your images and organize them into categories and tags
- **Excalidraw Integration**: Drag and drop your images into the Excalidraw window to create scenes and notes
- **Search Functionality**: Quickly find your images using the search and filter options
